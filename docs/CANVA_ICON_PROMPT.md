# Canva AI Prompt for Agora App Icon

  Create a minimalist app icon for "Agora" - a philosophical debate platform.

## SPECIFICATIONS

- Format: 1024x1024px square
- Style: Minimalist, thoughtful, classical aesthetic
- Must be recognizable and clear at 64x64px size
- Clean, uncluttered design with strong visual hierarchy

## DESIGN ELEMENTS

  Choose ONE primary element (or a subtle combination):

- An ancient Greek scroll (partially unrolled) with elegant lines
- A classical quill pen with a single feather
- A simplified Greek column (Doric or Ionic style) with clean geometric lines
- OR a subtle combination: scroll with quill resting on it, or column with scroll at base

## COLOR PALETTE (use these exact colors)

- Primary: Deep blue #1E3A5F (main element)
- Accent: Gold #D4AF37 (highlight or detail - use sparingly)
- Secondary: Warm gray #6B7280 (subtle shadows or secondary elements)
- Background: Light #FAFAFA or white #FFFFFF
- Text/Details: Dark #1A1A1A (if text is needed)

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

- Minimalist approach: single focal element, not cluttered
- Strong contrast for small-size visibility (64x64px)
- Geometric, clean lines - avoid intricate details
- Classical, timeless feel that suggests wisdom and discourse
- Subtle depth using soft shadows (warm gray #6B7280 at low opacity)
- Optional: very subtle gold accent (#D4AF37) as a small highlight or detail
- No text or lettering - icon should be purely symbolic
- Rounded corners consideration: design should work with iOS/Android icon corner radius

## COMPOSITION

- Center the main element
- Generous negative space around the element
- Element should occupy approximately 60-70% of the canvas
- Balanced, symmetrical or intentionally asymmetrical but harmonious

## MOOD & AESTHETIC

- Thoughtful, contemplative, scholarly
- Classical Greek/Roman inspiration
- Modern minimalism meets ancient wisdom
- Professional yet approachable
- Suggests dialogue, debate, and intellectual exchange

## TECHNICAL NOTES

- Ensure the icon reads clearly at small sizes (test mentally at 64x64px)
- Avoid fine details that disappear when scaled down
- Use bold, clear shapes and lines
- High contrast between foreground and background
- Consider how it will look on both light and dark backgrounds (if applicable)
